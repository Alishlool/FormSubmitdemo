About Us Who We Are

We are Multiservices company based in Istanbul, Turkey, offering our service to business channels in GCC and middle East countries.​
Afak Link coming from Arabic Language (Afak - Horizon) Our company established to connect the Business Horizons between East & West , Delivering the Latest Technologies , Solutions , IT Products , Finest goods from its Origin land. ​
Afak Link Team has wide experience and expertise in ICT and Smart technology, Enabling us to understand our customer needs and implementing the latest evolving technologies . ​
Afak Link believes in nature, Continuously discovering the origin of richest natural sources and deliver to our valued customers the best taste and quality products of Turkey .​
